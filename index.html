The Why

Our Children Need More Than Schooling

وَعَلِّمُوا أَوْلَادَكُمْ وَأَدِّبُوهُمْ
— "Teach your children and instil good character in them." — Prophet ﷺ (Ibn Majah)

Post-COVID, our children are more digitally connected and more socially disconnected. Schools focus on academics. Homes are stretched. The PreTeen years — ages 6 to 13 — are the most critical window for shaping values, identity and character.

Yet most neighbourhoods have no structured space for this sacred work. Noor Circles fills this gap. A small, intimate, weekly circle where children grow together — not just in knowledge, but in soul and light.

🌟

Aqeedah & Identity

Building a firm, confident Islamic identity before the world tries to define them. Faith rooted in love, not fear.

💎

Akhlaq & Character

Nurturing Prophetic virtues — honesty, courage, compassion, gratitude — through stories, reflection and lived practice.

🤲

Ibadah & Habits

Age-appropriate, joyful introduction to Salah, Quran and Dhikr. Making Deen beloved, not burdensome.

🕌

Community & Belonging

Children of different schools and backgrounds bonding as one Ummah — overcoming division from childhood.

The Noor Circle Model

Intimate. Neighbourhood-Based.
Deeply Transformative.

Designed for PreTeen children aged 6–13, each Noor Circle is led by a trained mentor — a small group, walking distance from home, every week.

🏘️

Neighbourhood-First

No long commutes. Every circle is within walking distance. Children from the same street, different schools, all backgrounds — together under one Noor.

Proximity Model
👥

Small & Intimate

Maximum 8–10 children per circle. The Prophet's ﷺ gatherings were intimate. Every child is known, every voice is heard, every heart is touched.

Optimal Group Size
📖

Structured Curriculum

A fully designed, age-appropriate Tarbiyah curriculum — not improvised. Every session has clear objectives, activities and beautiful takeaways.

Comprehensive Plan
📅

Weekly Consistency

2.5 hours, once a week. Enough to build relationship and habit. Light enough to sustain. Consistent enough to create lasting transformation.

Sustainable Rhythm
🧑‍🏫

Trained Mentors

Every mentor completes our comprehensive training — child psychology, Islamic Tarbiyah methodology, facilitation, and our full curriculum delivery system.

Full Training Support
📱

EdTech Powered

Our management portal handles attendance, progress tracking, parent communication, curriculum delivery and mentor support — beautifully in one place.
